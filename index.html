This is an interactive visualization of crime distribution across geography, location, time, and category in the city of Chicago during the years of the COVID-19 pandemic and the following years (2020-2023). To accomplish this, we preprocessedpublic City of Chicago crime data from 2001-present, parsing only the years 2020-2023 and only processing crimes with geographical data. We chose to focus on geographic variables (latitude and longitude), crime type (primary crime type), and crime location description (location description) as well as the time of the arrest (year and date).
