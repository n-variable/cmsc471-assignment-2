This is an interactive visualization of crime distribution across geography, location, time, and category during the years of the COVID-19 pandemic and the following years (2020-2023). To accomplish this, we used public City of Chicago crime data from 2001-present, parsing only the years 2020-2023. We chose to focus on geographic variables (latitude and longitude), crime type (primary crime type), and crime location description (location description) as well as the time of the arrest (year).
